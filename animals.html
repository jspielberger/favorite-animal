The Spider monkey, otherwise known as Simia paniscus, is an especially agile primate.

They are known as the smartest monkeys in the New World. Their long tails and limbs are what gave them their popular name. These lanky features are also what allow the Spider Monkey to live in and traverse through the upper layers of the rainforest
